About...

Queensgate is Peterborough city centres main shopping centre, home to over 90 stores including John Lewis, M&S, Bhs, Next, Boots, H&M, Primark, Superdry, River Island, Monsoon, Paperchase and many more. If you're looking for somewhere to stop for a bite to eat or a coffee you'll find lots of choice in the centre, eateries include Handmade Burger Co and Carluccio's, Costa Coffee, Muffin Break, The John Lewis Place To Eat, Cafe Revive at M&S, McDonalds, Pret and Greggs.

In addition to the main malls Queensgate is also home to the cities Westgate Arcade. The arcade dates back to the 1920s and is home to a varied mix of independent retailers including boutique opticians, award winning butchers and jewellers.

Queensgate is open weekdays until 5.30pm, with late night shopping until 8pm on Thursdays and Fridays, Saturdays until 6pm and on Sundays between 10.30 and 4.30. Queensgate is easy to get to, it's home to Peterboroughs bus station, only two minutes walk from the cities rail station and has four car parks with room for 2300 cars.
